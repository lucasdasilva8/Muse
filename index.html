Our Story

Born at Brown University, Muse started with a simple belief: talented musicians deserve better funding options, and fans deserve to be more than just listeners.

We're building a new ecosystem where creators and supporters grow together, creating lasting value for everyone involved. Join us in revolutionizing how music gets funded.
